Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a senior developer to do this?
No. You need basic technical literacy (knowing how to deploy an app or hire a cheap freelancer on Upwork for 2 hours to do it for you). This is a marketing and operations playbook, not a coding tutorial.
How much capital do I need to start?
You can acquire your first distressed asset for between $300 and $1,000. Phase 1 of the playbook teaches you how to find these off-market deals before they get marked up on broker sites.
Does this work outside the US (Stripe issues)?
Yes. In fact, Phase 2 includes a dedicated section on how international buyers (Middle East, Asia, Europe) can navigate payment gateways by bypassing Stripe and using Merchant of Record (MoR) solutions like Lemon Squeezy or Paddle.

Before you spend your money on your first acquisition, you will send me the details of the deal. I will record a private 10-minute Loom video reviewing the codebase, the traffic, and the red flags, giving you a definitive "Buy" or "Pass" recommendation.
