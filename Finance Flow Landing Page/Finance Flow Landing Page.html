Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data secure? ▼
Absolutely. FinanceFlow uses bank-grade encryption (AES-256) to secure your data. We do not connect directly to your bank credentials, ensuring your underlying accounts are never at risk.
How does the WhatsApp integration work? ▼
Once you enable it in your Premium settings, our verified WhatsApp business account will send you scheduled reminders for bills and weekly summaries. You can reply to these messages to quickly mark bills as paid.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes. You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time directly from your account settings. You will retain access to Premium features until the end of your billing cycle.
Do I have to download this from the App Store? ▼
No! FinanceFlow is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You simply click "Get Started," sign up, and follow the prompt to add the app directly to your phone's home screen. It works natively without taking up storage space.
